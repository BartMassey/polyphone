POLYPHONE


Sf2 files allow samples storage along with numerous parameters in order to create instruments driven by MIDI signals and readable by synthetisers using wavetables. These files contain a data structure made of 3 principal elements:

  • samples, coming from a trompet or a piano for instance,
  • instruments, made of samples,
  • presets, made of instruments.
Further to these elements definition and setting, a sf2 file contains also general informations, mainly about its origin.

POLYPHONE software has for first goal to create and edit .sf2 files, versions 2.01 and 2.04. It is made of:

  • a menu bar,
  • a toolbar,
  • a left part containing a tree showing all loaded sf2 files and their different components,
  • a right part allowing the edition of the selected element in the tree.

The right part offers several interfaces allowing:

  • the edition of the general informations of the file,
  • the edition of the samples,
  • the edition of the instruments,
  • the edition of the presets.

The second goal of POLYPHONE is to offer tools, accessible through the menu, facilitating and automating the edition of various parameters.
