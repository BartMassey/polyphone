Polyphone has been developed to answer several needs:

  • to propose a simple and ergonomic interface editing sf2 soundfonts (versions 2.01 and 2.04),
  • to deal efficiently with a large set of parameters,
  • to be available on several OS (currently running on Windows, Mac OS X and Linux).
The software is made of a left part comprising a tree showing all loaded sf2 files and their different components, and a right part for editing the selected item in the tree.

The right part offers several interfaces allowing the editing of:

  • the general information of a file,
  • the samples,
  • the instruments,
  • the presets.
Tools facilitating and automating several tasks are accessible through the menu bar.
