To facilitate the search for items in the tree, a search bar is available to filter.

When a string is inserted into the search bar:

  • if the string is included in the name of an instrument or a preset, instruments and presets are displayed as well as their divisions,
  • if the string is included in the name of a sample, the samples concerned as well as all instruments containing these samples are shown.
A click on the cross to the right of the editable field cancels the filter.

The tree can also be completely collapsed or expanded thaks to the two buttons on the left.
