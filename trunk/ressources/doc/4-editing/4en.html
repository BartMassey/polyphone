Several editing pages are available according to the element selected in the tree:

  • the editing page of the general information appears if the selection comprises the global header of a soundfont, one of its headers "samples", "instruments" or "presets", or elements having nothing in common,
  • the editing page of the samples appears if one or more samples are selected,
  • the editing page of instruments appears if one or more elements relative to an instrument (the instrument itself and / or one or more of its divisions) are selected,
  • the editing page of presets appears if one or more elements relative to a preset (the preset itself and / or one or more of its divisions) are selected,
If no elements are selected or if the selection comprises different soundfonts, the software icon is displayed.
