PRESETS


When clicking on a preset or an instrument linked to a preset, the page of preset edition appears. A preset allows the combination of several instruments, and is the element used by the synthesizer.

Like the page of instrument edition, two parts are present:

  • the table,
  • the modulator section (which can be displayed or hidden via the menu "View").
The parameters edited here are said relative unlike parameters of an instrument which, except in special cases, are said absolute. Values of a preset parameters are added to the instruments parameters that compose it. Some parameters are not editable at the preset level:
  • loop playback,
  • root key,
  • exclusive class,
  • fixed key,
  • fixed velocity,
  • the 4 offsets.

A preset is identified by a preset number and bank number, this combination being unique and adjustable above the table.
